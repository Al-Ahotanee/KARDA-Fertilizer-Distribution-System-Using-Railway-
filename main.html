About KARDA FDS

KARDA FDS, powered by cutting-edge blockchain technology, is revolutionizing fertilizer distribution in Jigawa State. As part of the Katsina Agricultural and Rural Development Authority, we ensure transparency, fairness, and efficiency for local farmers.

Our platform integrates AI-driven allocation, QR code verification, and real-time analytics to empower Katsina farmers, reduce waste, and boost agricultural productivity across the state.
